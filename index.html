Server Rules

Community Conduct

  • • Respect all players and staff
  • • No toxicity, harassment, hate speech, or bullying
  • • No advertising or spamming in chat
  • • Respect staff decisions at all times

Gameplay Integrity

  • • No hacking, cheating, or unfair advantages
  • • No exploits, dupes, or bug abuse
  • • No griefing, stealing, or damaging builds
  • • Spawn builds are strictly protected
  • • No lag machines or intentional lag
  • • Logging into another player’s account is punishable
  • • Alt grinding is not allowed
  • • Seed cracking is prohibited (season ban)

Mods & Client Rules

  • • Any player-tracking mods are NOT allowed (radar, ESP, entity tracking, etc.)
  • • Mods giving unfair PvP, combat, or survival advantages are banned
  • • Replay Mod recording is REQUIRED for reports or defense
